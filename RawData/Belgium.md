# Belgium

## Country status

Belgium is a critical semiconductor-spin enabler country through imec's 300mm CMOS quantum-dot process development.

## Government policy and support

- Belgium participates in EU quantum-policy coordination and supports imec-centered deep-tech R&D structures.

## Universities and research institutions

- imec and partner institutions in Leuven are central to industrially manufacturable spin-qubit process development.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- imec (R&D hub)
- Strategic collaborations with Diraq and other international spin-qubit teams.

## Standout milestones

- imec reported record-low charge noise on 300mm CMOS-compatible silicon quantum-dot devices.
