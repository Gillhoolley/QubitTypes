# Ireland

## Country status

Ireland is a fast-rising semiconductor-spin country via Equal1 and a clear national quantum strategy.

## Government policy and support

- Quantum 2030 (Ireland national strategy) provides policy direction.

## Universities and research institutions

- University College Dublin (UCD) spinout ecosystem and quantum research participation.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Equal1 (silicon-based quantum server/platform)
- Collaborations include NVIDIA and Irish industry/research partners.

## Standout milestones

- Equal1 launched Bell-1 (Irish-made silicon quantum server) and raised additional funding for rollout.
