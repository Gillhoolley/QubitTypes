# Semiconductor Spin Country Rating Index (SSCRI-2026)

Date: 2026-03-07

## Framework

Countries are scored 1-5 on six dimensions and converted to a weighted 0-100 score.

Weights:
- `Policy & Funding` 25%
- `Research Depth` 20%
- `Industry Strength` 20%
- `Deployment Infrastructure` 15%
- `Global Integration` 10%
- `Commercial Momentum` 10%

Formula:
`Score = ((P*0.25 + R*0.20 + I*0.20 + D*0.15 + G*0.10 + C*0.10) / 5) * 100`

Rating bands:
- 85-100: Global Leader
- 70-84: Strong Contender
- 55-69: Emerging Power
- 40-54: Developing Ecosystem
- <40: Early Stage
