# United States

## Country status

The US leads globally in semiconductor spin-qubit commercialization via Intel and deep federal-research infrastructure.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Initiative Act remains the core federal framework.
- DOE QIS Centers and related federal programs continue to fund scalable quantum hardware research.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Maryland/LPS ecosystem, Sandia, Wisconsin, Rochester and others via QCF participation.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Intel (Tunnel Falls and follow-on silicon spin programs)

## Standout milestones

- Intel released Tunnel Falls, a 12-qubit silicon spin research chip fabricated on 300mm wafers.
