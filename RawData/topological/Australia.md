# Australia

## Country status

Australia is an emerging topological-quantum ecosystem with strong national policy support and active university research, but limited topological-qubit commercialization so far.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Strategy (2023) provides long-term policy framework.
- ARC centres and federal programs continue to fund quantum hardware and advanced materials research.

## Universities and research institutions

- UNSW and partner institutions are active in Majorana/topological-qubit theory and decoherence research.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Commercial activity is strong in broader quantum, while dedicated topological-qubit company activity remains early.

## Standout milestones

- New ARC quantum center funding (2025) and continued Majorana-qubit research publications/presentations.
