# United States

## Country status

The US is the global center of topological-qubit commercialization attempts, led by Microsoft.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Initiative Act provides the federal framework.
- DARPA's quantum benchmarking programs advanced Microsoft's topological program into later-stage evaluation tracks.

## Universities and research institutions

- UCSB (StationQ lineage), University of Maryland, MIT and major federal-lab ecosystem.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Microsoft (Majorana/topological qubit program)

## Standout milestones

- Microsoft announced **Majorana 1** (February 19, 2025) and a topological-qubit roadmap.
