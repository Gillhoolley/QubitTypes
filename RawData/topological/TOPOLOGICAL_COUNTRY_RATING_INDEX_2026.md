# Topological Country Rating Index (TQCRI-2026)

Date: 2026-03-07

## Framework

Each country is scored 1-5 and converted to a weighted 0-100 score.

Weights:
- `Policy & Funding` 25%
- `Research Depth` 25%
- `Industry Presence` 15%
- `Validation/Deployment` 15%
- `Global Integration` 10%
- `Commercial Momentum` 10%

Formula:
`Score = ((P*0.25 + R*0.25 + I*0.15 + D*0.15 + G*0.10 + C*0.10) / 5) * 100`

Rating bands:
- 85-100: Global Leader
- 70-84: Strong Contender
- 55-69: Emerging Power
- 40-54: Developing Ecosystem
- <40: Early Stage

## Interpretation

- This index measures **ecosystem readiness for topological-qubit progress**, not guaranteed near-term product outcomes.
- The field is pre-scale: scientific validation milestones can significantly shift scores year-to-year.
