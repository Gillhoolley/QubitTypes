# Japan

## Country status

Japan is a strong topological-quantum research country with major university and national-lab contributions plus national quantum policy support.

## Government policy and support

- MEXT Q-LEAP and NEDO programs support advanced quantum research and ecosystem growth.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Tokyo, RIKEN, and other top institutions are active in Majorana/topological superconductivity research.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Industrial ecosystem is more active in broader quantum modalities; topological-qubit work remains research-heavy.

## Standout milestones

- University of Tokyo teams reported new Majorana-related spintronics/topological findings (2025).
