# China

## Country status

China has strong topological quantum research depth, especially in Majorana-related experiments and simulations, with strategic national policy support.

## Government policy and support

- Quantum information science remains a strategic priority in national planning narratives and CAS communications.

## Universities and research institutions

- USTC, CAS Institute of Physics, and related national labs lead major projects.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Publicly visible topological-qubit commercialization is less concentrated in globally visible private vendors than in the US model; activity is strongly institution-led.

## Standout milestones

- USTC reported Jones polynomial calculations based on simulated braided Majorana zero modes (2025).
- CAS-reported advances in large-scale/tunable Majorana-zero-mode lattice work.
