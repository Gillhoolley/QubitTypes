# Germany

## Country status

Germany is a major European topological-quantum enabler through deep public funding and strong topological materials research clusters.

## Government policy and support

- Federal Handlungskonzept Quantentechnologien and large multi-year public investments.
- Excellence-strategy clusters support topological quantum materials work.

## Universities and research institutions

- Wurzburg-Dresden topological materials cluster (ctd.qmat).
- Broad federal-university-research institute network.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Company activity is mostly indirect (materials, devices, and quantum ecosystem services) rather than mature topological-qubit hardware vendors.

## Standout milestones

- Significant progress in topological superconductivity/materials findings relevant to Majorana-based computing pathways.
