# United Kingdom

## Country status

The UK is a strong quantum-policy country and an active topological-enabling research environment, though topological-qubit commercialization remains early.

## Government policy and support

- UK National Quantum Strategy and National Quantum Technologies Programme provide long-term support.

## Universities and research institutions

- Oxford and other UK universities are active in topological superconductivity and Majorana-related research.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Fewer UK-headquartered companies are dedicated to topological qubits specifically; most commercialization is currently in other qubit modalities.

## Standout milestones

- University of Oxford reported Andreev-STM-related topological superconductivity advances relevant to topological QC materials discovery.
