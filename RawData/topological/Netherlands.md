# Netherlands

## Country status

The Netherlands is one of the world's most important topological-qubit research hubs, especially through Delft/QuTech.

## Government policy and support

- Quantum Delta NL is the national ecosystem program with major public support and explicit commercialization goals.

## Universities and research institutions

- TU Delft / QuTech remains central to Majorana-qubit materials and device research.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Company activity is still mostly ecosystem-adjacent; topological-qubit commercialization itself remains research-centric.
- Strong public-private innovation pipeline across Dutch quantum companies.

## Standout milestones

- Continued national-scale funding and global ranking improvements in quantum ecosystem performance.
