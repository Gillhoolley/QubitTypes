# Switzerland

## Country status

Switzerland is an emerging topological-quantum country with strong foundational research in topological superconductivity and Majorana physics.

## Government policy and support

- The Swiss Quantum Initiative (SQI) is federally mandated and coordinates national quantum priorities across research, innovation, and infrastructure.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Basel teams report direct signatures of Majorana bound states in lead-based superconducting structures.
- ETH Zurich and PSI participate in national quantum ecosystem buildout and advanced device research.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Switzerland's topological activity is currently research-led; large dedicated topological-qubit commercialization remains early-stage.

## Standout milestones

- Basel-led work identifying topological superconducting phases with Majorana states.
