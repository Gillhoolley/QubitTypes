# United Kingdom

## Country status

The UK is a top commercialization market for semiconductor spin qubits, centered on Quantum Motion and NQCC testbed deployment.

## Government policy and support

- UK National Quantum Strategy underpins long-term policy.
- Government quantum funding in 2024-2025 expanded prototype/testbed and adoption pathways.

## Universities and research institutions

- UCL/Oxford-linked origins of Quantum Motion.
- NQCC as national testbed and validation platform.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Quantum Motion (silicon CMOS spin-qubit platform)

## Standout milestones

- Quantum Motion delivered a full-stack silicon CMOS quantum computer to NQCC in 2025.
