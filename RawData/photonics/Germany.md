# Germany

## Role in photonic quantum

Germany is a major European deployment market and funding center for quantum technologies, including photonic systems procurement and integration.

## Government policy and support

- Federal Handlungskonzept Quantentechnologien and multi-billion-euro federal support framework.

## Universities and research institutions

- DLR and German research institutes are key users and integrators of multiple quantum modalities.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- QuiX Quantum has sold photonic systems to DLR and operates in Germany (Ulm, Stuttgart).
- Germany attracts international quantum companies through industrial and aerospace demand.
