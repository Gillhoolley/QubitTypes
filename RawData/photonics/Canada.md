# Canada

## Role in photonic quantum

Canada is one of the top global photonic quantum countries, combining a strong national strategy with world-leading photonic companies.

## Government policy and support

- Canada launched the National Quantum Strategy in 2023.
- In 2025, Canada announced the Canadian Quantum Champions Program and direct support including Xanadu and Photonic.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Waterloo's Institute for Quantum Computing and other hubs in Ontario, Quebec, Alberta, and BC.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Xanadu (photonic quantum computing)
- Photonic (photonically linked architecture)
- Government-backed company cohort also includes Anyon Systems and Nord Quantique.

## Standout industry developments

- Xanadu opened a CA$10M advanced photonic packaging facility in Toronto (2025).
