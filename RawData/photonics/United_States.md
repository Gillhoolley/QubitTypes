# United States

## Role in photonic quantum

The US is a global frontrunner in photonic quantum commercialization, public-sector benchmarking, and defense-linked scaling programs.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Initiative framework continues to guide federal quantum coordination.
- DARPA's US2QC/QBI programs are explicitly evaluating utility-scale approaches, including photonic architectures.

## Universities and research institutions

- Strong federal lab and university network (NIST, DOE labs, university hubs).

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- PsiQuantum (US-headquartered; photonic, fault-tolerant roadmap)
- Broad US ecosystem support in manufacturing and government contracts

## Standout developments

- DARPA moved PsiQuantum into advanced program phases for utility-scale evaluation.
- PsiQuantum announced government-linked contracts and US deployment plans.
