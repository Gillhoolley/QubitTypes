# United Kingdom

## Role in photonic quantum

The UK is one of the strongest photonic quantum countries globally, combining national strategy funding with high-impact local companies.

## Government policy and support

- UK National Quantum Strategy (10-year framework).
- Government-backed testbeds and direct project funding for photonic systems.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Oxford spinout pipeline (ORCA)
- NQCC as national testbed center
- STFC/Daresbury and Hartree Centre links

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- ORCA Computing (UK photonic quantum hardware)
- PsiQuantum UK R&D site at Daresbury backed by UK government funding

## Standout deployments

- ORCA delivered the first photonic quantum testbed system to the UK NQCC (2025).
