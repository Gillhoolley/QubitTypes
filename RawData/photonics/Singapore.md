# Singapore

## Role in photonic quantum

Singapore is a highly coordinated quantum ecosystem with strong policy support for photonics-linked processor and secure-network development.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Strategy (2024) and National Quantum Office programs (NQCH, NQSN, NQFF, NQPI).
- Program portfolio explicitly includes photonics component and processor capabilities.

## Universities and research institutions

- CQT (NUS/NTU/SUTD/A*STAR network)
- National research programs managed under NQO/QEP

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- SpeQtral (quantum communications with photonics/QKD relevance)
- Toshiba-SpeQtral-ST Engineering collaboration in Singapore and Southeast Asia.
