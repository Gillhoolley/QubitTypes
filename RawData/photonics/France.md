# France

## Role in photonic quantum

France is a leading European photonic quantum country, with Quandela as a flagship company under France's national quantum strategy framework.

## Government policy and support

- France's national quantum strategy is embedded in France 2030 programs.
- HQI (France Hybrid HPC-Quantum Initiative) provides a public infrastructure path that includes photonic systems.

## Universities and research institutions

- CEA/CEA-Leti, Inria, and university ecosystem linked with HQI and national programs.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Quandela (full-stack photonic quantum computing)
- Public research-industry links through CEA-Leti and national infrastructure programs.
