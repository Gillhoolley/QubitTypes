# Israel

## Role in photonic quantum

Israel is an emerging photonic quantum country with strong startup intensity and government-backed quantum center infrastructure.

## Government policy and support

- Israel Innovation Authority continues to run quantum-focused international and domestic R&D support tracks.

## Universities and research institutions

- Tel Aviv University-linked national center activity and deep university research base (including Weizmann-origin optical work).

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Quantum Source (photonic quantum computing startup, significant private funding)
- Quantum Machines-led center model has included photonic processor participation.
