# South Korea

## Role in photonic quantum

South Korea is a fast-scaling quantum country with strong government roadmap activity and increasing integration of international systems.

## Government policy and support

- MSIT announced a 2035-oriented quantum industrial roadmap in early 2026.
- National quantum center and hybrid HPC-quantum integration model is being developed.

## Universities and research institutions

- Yonsei University, KISTI, ETRI, and KAIST-linked collaborations.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- IonQ partnership and system delivery track with KISTI.
- Domestic optical communications players and ETRI-linked photonic-QKD component transfer activity.
