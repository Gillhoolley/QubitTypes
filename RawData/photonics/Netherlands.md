# Netherlands

## Role in photonic quantum

The Netherlands is a global photonic quantum leader, with strong university spinouts, public funding, and company scale-up momentum.

## Government policy and support

- Quantum Delta NL is nationally coordinated and heavily funded through Dutch and EU mechanisms.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Twente and other Dutch hubs (Delft, Amsterdam, Leiden, Eindhoven) are central.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- QuiX Quantum (leading photonic quantum hardware company)
- Strong investor and public-fund participation (Invest-NL, EIC Fund, regional funds).

## Standout photonic innovations

- QuiX announced Series A in 2025 to deliver a universal single-photon photonic computer.
