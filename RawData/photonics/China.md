# China

## Role in photonic quantum

China is a global leader in photonic quantum demonstrations, especially through USTC/CAS work on Jiuzhang.

## Government policy and support

- Public messaging from CAS indicates strong quantum development momentum through the 14th Five-Year Plan period and continued priority in the 15th.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)
- Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS)

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Industrial ecosystem includes companies across superconducting and photonic routes (policy-linked industrialization messaging continues to expand).

## Standout photonic innovations

- Jiuzhang photonic quantum computing line (including later-generation demonstrations).
