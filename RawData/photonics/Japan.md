# Japan

## Role in photonic quantum

Japan is a top-tier quantum country with strong optical-photonic capabilities, major telecom/industrial players, and coordinated national programs.

## Government policy and support

- Japan's national quantum policy architecture combines government programs with large industrial participation.

## Universities and research institutions

- RIKEN, AIST, NICT, University of Tokyo, Osaka University, and other national-university partners.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- NTT has active optical quantum programs and signed a 2025 collaboration with OptQC targeting scalable optical quantum systems.
- Toshiba is active in quantum-secure photonic communications in Asia.
