# Australia

## Role in photonic quantum

Australia is a major deployment destination for photonic fault-tolerant ambitions, mainly through large public backing for PsiQuantum.

## Government policy and support

- Australia published a National Quantum Strategy and aligned major investment actions.
- Federal and Queensland governments committed a large package to support PsiQuantum buildout in Brisbane.

## Universities and research institutions

- University and research collaboration is embedded in the Brisbane program.
- Australia also has a broad quantum research base (UNSW and others), though not all of it is photonic.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- PsiQuantum (US-origin company) selected Brisbane for Asia-Pacific HQ and utility-scale development program.
