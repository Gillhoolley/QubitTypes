# Germany

## Why Germany leads in trapped-ion

Germany combines major federal quantum funding with one of the world's largest public procurement tracks for trapped-ion systems.

## Government policy and support

- Federal Handlungskonzept Quantentechnologien backed by multi-billion-euro funding.
- BMWK/DLR programs placed major orders for ion-trap systems.

## Universities and research institutions

- DLR innovation centers in Hamburg/Ulm and multiple partner universities.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Universal Quantum Deutschland (DLR contracts)
- eleQtron-led consortium projects (QSea I/II)
- QUDORA trapped-ion systems and industrial collaborations
