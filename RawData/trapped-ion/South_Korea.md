# South Korea

## Why South Korea is a strong contender

South Korea is scaling trapped-ion deployment rapidly through national center planning and major international-company integration.

## Government policy and support

- MSIT announced a 2035-oriented quantum industry roadmap and cluster plan in 2026.
- National Quantum Computing Center of Excellence framework includes trapped-ion infrastructure.

## Universities and research institutions

- KISTI
- Yonsei University and national research users linked through hybrid platforms

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- IonQ selected as primary partner with KISTI, including a 100-qubit Tempo system delivery track.
