# United Kingdom

## Why the UK leads in trapped-ion

The UK has one of the strongest trapped-ion commercialization clusters globally, combining national strategy, NQCC testbeds, and high-value spinouts.

## Government policy and support

- UK National Quantum Strategy (10-year framework).
- NQCC/Innovate UK testbed funding includes trapped-ion systems.
- UK government issued a final order on IonQ's acquisition of Oxford Ionics under NSI rules (2025).

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Oxford spinout ecosystem (Oxford Ionics).
- University of Sussex heritage via Universal Quantum.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Oxford Ionics (now acquired by IonQ)
- Quantinuum UK presence (Cambridge, London, Oxford)
- Universal Quantum (UK company with major Germany build contracts)
