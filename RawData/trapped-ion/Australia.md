# Australia

## Why Australia is an emerging trapped-ion ecosystem

Australia has strong national quantum policy support and visible trapped-ion research capability in universities.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Strategy (2023) provides long-term policy framework for quantum commercialization and infrastructure.
- Follow-on funding programs support quantum growth and demand creation.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Sydney has demonstrated trapped-ion-based quantum simulation work in its Nanoscience Hub.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Australia has strong quantum company activity overall, but trapped-ion commercialization is currently more research-led than company-led in public disclosures.
