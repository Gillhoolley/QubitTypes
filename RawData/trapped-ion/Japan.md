# Japan

## Why Japan is a trapped-ion leader

Japan has a strong policy-backed hybrid-HPC model and has deployed a major on-prem trapped-ion system at RIKEN.

## Government policy and support

- MEXT Q-LEAP programs and METI/NEDO-backed projects support quantum development.
- RIKEN trapped-ion deployment is part of a NEDO-commissioned national project.

## Universities and research institutions

- RIKEN
- University of Tokyo
- Osaka University

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Quantinuum deployed trapped-ion system "Reimei" at RIKEN.
- SoftBank participates in the hybrid quantum-HPC program.
