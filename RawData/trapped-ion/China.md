# China

## Why China is strategically important in trapped-ion

China has very strong trapped-ion academic capability and increasing policy support under national quantum priorities.

## Government policy and support

- 14th Five-Year planning documents and official commentary identify quantum information as a strategic priority area.
- CAS commentary indicates continuing support and industry-academia integration into the 15th Five-Year period.

## Universities and research institutions

- Tsinghua University (Prof. Luming Duan's group)
- USTC and CAS-linked institutions

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Activity remains largely research-institution-led in public English sources, with fewer globally visible trapped-ion hardware startups than in US/UK/Austria/Germany.

## Standout achievements

- Tsinghua reported world-leading scale trapped-ion simulation experiments (hundreds of ion qubits in simulation contexts).
