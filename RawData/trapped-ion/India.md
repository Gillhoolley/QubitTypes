# India

## Why India is strategically important

India's national mission explicitly supports multiple hardware modalities including trapped ions, creating long-term policy runway for trapped-ion R&D.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Mission (NQM) includes trapped-ion as a focus hardware platform in mission design.

## Universities and research institutions

- IISER Pune has an active ion-trap effort targeting multi-qubit trapped-ion capability.
- Broader NQM ecosystem includes IITs, IISERs, and national labs.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Publicly visible trapped-ion commercialization remains early-stage compared with software and cybersecurity startups.
- Near-term progress remains institution-led under NQM.
