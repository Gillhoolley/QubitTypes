# United States

## Why the US leads in trapped-ion

The US combines deep federal research support with leading commercial trapped-ion companies and strong university pipelines.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Initiative Act provides the federal coordination framework.
- DOE National QIS Research Centers and NSF ExpandQISE strengthen hardware/software/workforce depth.

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Maryland and Duke are foundational to IonQ's trapped-ion lineage.
- NIST runs multiple trapped-ion programs (computing, networking, simulation/sensing).

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- IonQ (US headquartered; core trapped-ion platform)
- Quantinuum major US footprint (Broomfield, Minnesota, DC)
