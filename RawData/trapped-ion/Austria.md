# Austria

## Why Austria leads in trapped-ion

Austria has one of the world's deepest trapped-ion research roots (Innsbruck) and a strong commercialization path through AQT.

## Government policy and support

- Quantum Austria invests EUR 107 million through 2026 (NextGenerationEU-backed national program).

## Universities and research institutions

- University of Innsbruck is a globally recognized trapped-ion center.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- AQT (Alpine Quantum Technologies), Innsbruck-based trapped-ion hardware company.
- AQT systems are available on cloud channels including Amazon Braket.
