# Switzerland

## Why Switzerland is a strong contender

Switzerland has a coordinated national quantum initiative and one of Europe's strongest trapped-ion academic infrastructures.

## Government policy and support

- Swiss Quantum Initiative (SQI), mandated through federal structures, with dedicated funding lines and innovation calls.

## Universities and research institutions

- ETH Zurich-PSI Quantum Computing Hub includes a dedicated trapped-ion team.
- PSI Ion Trap Quantum Computing group building open testbed capability.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- ZuriQ (ETH spinout, trapped-ion scalability focus, early-stage funding).
