# France

## Country status

France is emerging as a major semiconductor-spin commercialization center in Europe through Quobly and CEA-Leti-linked industrialization.

## Government policy and support

- France's national quantum policy under France 2030 supports hardware commercialization pathways.

## Universities and research institutions

- CEA-Leti and CNRS are key research foundations behind Quobly.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Quobly (silicon spin qubits on FD-SOI)
- Partnerships with STMicroelectronics and international research/foundry collaborators

## Standout milestones

- Quobly launched as CEA-Leti/CNRS spinout and expanded industrialization collaborations in 2025-2026.
