# Japan

## Country status

Japan has strong semiconductor-spin research depth and national-program support, with commercialization still earlier-stage than US/UK/Australia.

## Government policy and support

- MEXT Q-LEAP and JST programs continue funding spin-qubit and semiconductor quantum research.

## Universities and research institutions

- RIKEN and University of Tokyo-linked groups are key in silicon spin qubit progress.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Hitachi reported silicon-qubit control/lifetime improvements.
- Broader Japanese industry remains active in multiple qubit modalities.

## Standout milestones

- RIKEN demonstrated silicon spin-qubit error-correction primitives and three-qubit control.
