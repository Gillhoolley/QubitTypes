# Netherlands

## Country status

The Netherlands is a world-class semiconductor-spin research and scale-up ecosystem, especially through QuTech and national ecosystem funding.

## Government policy and support

- Quantum Delta NL is heavily backed by National Growth Fund and EU funding tracks.

## Universities and research institutions

- QuTech (TU Delft + TNO) is a global hub for semiconductor-spin research.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Intel-QuTech long-term collaboration extension.
- Dutch ecosystem supports spin-qubit and cryo-CMOS advancement and startup pipelines.

## Standout milestones

- QuTech demonstrated larger germanium spin-qubit arrays in 2025.
