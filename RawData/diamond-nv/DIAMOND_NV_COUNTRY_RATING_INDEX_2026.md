# Diamond NV Center Quantum Innovation Country Rating Index (DNVRI-2026)
Date: 2026-03-09
## Method
The index score combines five dimensions on a 1-5 scale:
1. Policy support
2. Research strength
3. Industry maturity
4. Deployment readiness
5. Global position
Countries are grouped into:
- Global Leader
- Strong Contender
- Emerging Power
