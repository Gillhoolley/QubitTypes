# Finland

## Country status

Finland is an emerging semiconductor-spin and cryo-CMOS innovation base, with strong VTT spinout activity.

## Government policy and support

- Finland's national quantum strategy (2025-2035) sets long-term direction.

## Universities and research institutions

- VTT and Finnish applied-research institutions support semiconductor-spin translation.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- SemiQon (silicon quantum and cryo-CMOS technologies, VTT spinout)

## Standout milestones

- SemiQon won EARTO recognition with VTT for cryogenic CMOS innovation relevant to scalable spin-qubit systems.
