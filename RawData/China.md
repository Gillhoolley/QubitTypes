# China

## Country status

China has strong semiconductor-spin academic activity and strategic policy backing, while globally visible private semiconductor-spin commercialization remains less concentrated.

## Government policy and support

- Quantum information remains a strategic priority in national planning channels and CAS communications.

## Universities and research institutions

- USTC and CAS institutions report ongoing silicon quantum-dot spin-qubit advances.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Publicly visible commercial momentum in semiconductor-spin is less startup-centric in international channels than in the US/Australia/Europe models.

## Standout milestones

- CAS/USTC reported silicon quantum-dot spin-qubit lifetime and control advances.
