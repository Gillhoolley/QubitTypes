# Australia

## Country status

Australia is a global leader in semiconductor spin qubits through deep university roots and globally relevant startups.

## Government policy and support

- National Quantum Strategy (2023) provides long-horizon policy support.
- Public policy emphasizes commercialization, infrastructure, and sovereign capability.

## Universities and research institutions

- UNSW Sydney is a major global center for silicon spin and donor-spin research.

## Companies investing and doing R&D

- Diraq (silicon quantum dot spin qubits)
- Silicon Quantum Computing (atomic-scale silicon qubits)

## Standout milestones

- Diraq reported high-fidelity CMOS-foundry-compatible spin-qubit results and advanced in DARPA QBI.
- SQC was selected for DARPA QBI and continues atomic-precision silicon platform progress.
